Project: Redesign the website for Rochelle & Associates, a long-standing law firm based in Lawton, Oklahoma.

Goal: Refresh the design to feel more hometown-friendly, personal, and trustworthy, while maintaining a clean, professional aesthetic suitable for a legal practice.

Creative Direction:

The client is a big Oklahoma Sooners fan and wants subtle nods to that Oklahoma pride and community feel — think crimson accents, warm neutral tones, and photography that reflects Lawton life (local landmarks, courthouse, Fort Sill, Oklahoma plains).

The site should feel approachable, not corporate — embody the sense of a neighbor you can trust when facing tough legal issues.

Use authentic imagery over stock photos: consider local courthouse steps, Oklahoma sunsets, handshake moments, or small-town street scenes.

Fonts: clean, readable serif or modern sans-serif pairing. Keep headlines confident but welcoming.

Layout: full-width hero image with a strong headline like “Protecting Lawton Families for Over 36 Years”. Include clear CTAs (“Call Now,” “Get a Free Consultation”).

Maintain professionalism — no sports logos, mascots, or overt fan imagery; the OU inspiration should stay subtle (through color palette, warmth, and tone).

Key Pages:

Home

Practice Areas (Criminal Defense, DUI Defense, Family Law, Tribal Law)

About Us (highlighting decades of service in Lawton, Oklahoma roots)

Contact (map, phone, consultation form)

Tone & Feel:

Professional yet personal

Rooted in Oklahoma community values

Trustworthy, experienced, local advocate

Approachable language — avoid corporate jargon

Design Inspiration Keywords:
“Oklahoma hometown law firm,” “Lawton community pride,” “Warm crimson and sandstone color palette,” “Professional but personal,” “Heritage and integrity.”

Technical Notes:

Keep SEO structure intact (existing URLs, headings, canonical tags, schema).

Optimize all images for web (small file size, descriptive alt-text).

Ensure mobile-first design and fast load times.

Preserve all current metadata and internal linking from existing SEO setup.
